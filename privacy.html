Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Welcome to our website! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our site.

1. What We Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal information unless you choose to contact us through our "Contact Us" form. In that case, we’ll only use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry. We do not store game scores, answers, or activity data.

2. Analytics & Tracking

We use Google Analytics to collect basic information about how visitors use the site, such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Browser type and general location (IP-based)

This helps us understand and improve our game.

3. Cookies & Advertising

We may use cookies (small files stored on your device) for:

  • Analytics
  • Remembering settings (if added later)
  • Personalized advertising

We display ads through Google AdSense, which may use cookies and other tracking technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing behavior.

You can learn more about how Google uses your data here.

4. User Submissions

You may email us language suggestions or ideas, but we do not collect this data through the website itself.

By emailing us, you agree that your suggestions may be used in the game or shared publicly. Please don’t send sensitive information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have received information from a child, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what data we have about you (if any)
  • Request deletion of your information

We will respond as quickly as possible.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

For any questions or privacy-related concerns, email us at: contactmichaelandjose@gmail.com
