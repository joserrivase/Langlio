About Us

Our Mission

Langlio is a fun and educational language guessing game designed to help people discover and learn about different languages from around the world. Our mission is to make language learning accessible, engaging, and enjoyable for everyone.

How It Works

Langlio presents players with sentences in various languages and challenges them to:

  • Guess the Language: Identify which language the sentence is written in
  • Discover Countries: Learn which countries speak that language
  • Expand Knowledge: Build awareness of global linguistic diversity

Educational Value

Our game helps players develop:

  • Language recognition skills
  • Geographic knowledge of language distribution
  • Cultural awareness and appreciation
  • Pattern recognition abilities

Features

  • Daily Challenges: New sentences every day to keep the game fresh
  • Hint System: Helpful clues when you're stuck
  • Progress Tracking: Save your game progress automatically
  • Mobile Friendly: Play on any device, anywhere
  • Share Results: Challenge friends to beat your score

Our Commitment

We're committed to creating an inclusive, educational, and entertaining experience that celebrates the rich diversity of languages and cultures around the world. Whether you're a language enthusiast, a student, or just curious about the world, Langlio is here to make your language discovery journey fun and rewarding.
