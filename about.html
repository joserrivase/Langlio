About Langlio

Langlio is a daily language guessing game with new languages every day. Every day, a new sentence is released, and it is your job to guess what language it is and where that language is from. After guessing correctly, you can pat yourself on the back, knowing you are more worldly than all of your friends. Still not convinced? Make sure to send them the game to see who is smarter.

Our Story

How It Works

Langlio presents players with sentences in various languages and challenges them to:

  • Guess the Language: Identify which language the sentence is written in
  • Discover Countries: Learn which countries speak that language
  • Expand Knowledge: Build awareness of global linguistic diversity
